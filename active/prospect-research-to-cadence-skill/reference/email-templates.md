## Anti-Patterns (Never Do)
- Don't open with "I hope this finds you well"
- Don't list features — lead with their pain
- Don't use more than 100 words
- Don't ask for "30 minutes" — always say "15 minutes"
- Don't mention pricing in cold outreach
- Don't use "synergy", "leverage", "touch base"
