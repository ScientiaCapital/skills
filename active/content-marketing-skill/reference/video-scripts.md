# Video Script Templates

Frameworks for B2B video content.

## YouTube Tutorial (5-10 min)

```markdown
# [How to [Outcome] - Step by Step Tutorial]

**Target length:** 7-10 minutes
**Goal:** Teach a skill, rank for search

---

## HOOK (0:00-0:15)
[Start with the transformation]
"By the end of this video, you'll know exactly how to [outcome]."

OR

[Start with the problem]
"Struggling with [problem]? You're not alone. [X%] of [audience] face this same issue."

---

## INTRO (0:15-0:45)
"Hey, I'm [Name] from [Company]. I help [audience] [achieve outcome]."

"Today I'm going to show you [what they'll learn] in [number] simple steps."

"If you find this helpful, hit subscribe - I put out [content type] every [frequency]."

---

## WHY THIS MATTERS (0:45-1:30)
[Context setting]
- Why learn this now?
- What happens if they don't?
- Quick story or example

---

## STEP 1: [Action] (1:30-3:00)
"The first thing you need to do is [action]."

[Show on screen]
- Walk through the process
- Explain why, not just what
- Point out common mistakes

"Pro tip: [Advanced insight]"

---

## STEP 2: [Action] (3:00-5:00)
"Now that you've [previous step], let's [next action]."

[Show on screen]
- Walk through the process
- Real example
- What success looks like

---

## STEP 3: [Action] (5:00-7:00)
"This is where most people get stuck. Here's the trick..."

[Show on screen]
- Address the tricky part
- Show alternative approaches
- Troubleshooting tips

---

## RECAP (7:00-7:30)
"Let's quickly recap what we covered:"
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

---

## CTA (7:30-8:00)
"If this helped, give it a thumbs up and subscribe for more [topic] content."

"I've got a [resource] linked in the description that goes even deeper on [topic]."

"Next video: [Teaser for related content]"

---

## END SCREEN (8:00-8:15)
[Point to subscribe button and suggested video]
```

## LinkedIn Native Video (60-90 sec)

```markdown
# [Punchy Topic or Question]

**Target length:** 60-90 seconds
**Goal:** Engagement, thought leadership

---

## HOOK (0:00-0:05)
[Pattern interrupt]
- "Stop doing [common mistake]."
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"
- "[Question that stops the scroll]"

---

## SETUP (0:05-0:15)
[Context]
"Here's what I mean..."
OR
"I learned this the hard way..."

---

## MAIN POINT (0:15-0:50)
[One clear idea]
- Point 1 (10 sec)
- Point 2 (10 sec)
- Point 3 (10 sec)

[Keep it conversational, not scripted]

---

## TAKEAWAY (0:50-1:10)
"The bottom line: [Key insight]"

---

## CTA (1:10-1:20)
"What do you think? Drop a comment."
OR
"Save this for later."
OR
"Follow for more [topic] content."
```

## Webinar/Presentation (30-45 min)

```markdown
# [Webinar Title: Promise of Value]

**Target length:** 30-45 minutes
**Goal:** Lead generation, deep education

---

## PRE-SHOW (5 min before)
"Hey everyone, we're starting in a few minutes. Drop where you're joining from in the chat!"

[Build energy, acknowledge people]

---

## OPENING (0:00-3:00)

### Hook
"By the end of this session, you'll have [specific outcome]."

### Credibility
"Quick background: I'm [Name], [credentials]. I've [relevant experience]."

### Agenda
"Here's what we're covering:
1. [Topic 1]
2. [Topic 2]
3. [Topic 3]
4. Q&A"

### Housekeeping
"Drop questions in the chat anytime. We'll have Q&A at the end."

---

## SECTION 1: [Topic] (3:00-12:00)

### The Problem
"Let me start with a story..."
[Relatable scenario or case study]

### The Insight
"Here's what most people don't realize..."
[Your unique perspective]

### The Framework
"I use a [number]-step approach called [Name]..."
[Visual/slide showing framework]

### Example
"Let me show you this in action..."
[Real example or demo]

### Engagement
"Quick question for the chat: [Related question]"

---

## SECTION 2: [Topic] (12:00-22:00)
[Same structure]

---

## SECTION 3: [Topic] (22:00-32:00)
[Same structure]

---

## RECAP (32:00-35:00)
"Let's pull this all together..."

[Summary slide]
- Key point 1
- Key point 2
- Key point 3

"If you remember nothing else: [One key takeaway]"

---

## CTA (35:00-38:00)
"Now, I mentioned [resource/offer] at the beginning..."

[Transition to offer]
- What it is
- Who it's for
- How to get it
- Urgency element (if genuine)

---

## Q&A (38:00-45:00)
"Let's open it up for questions. [Name], I see your question about..."

[Answer 3-5 questions]

"Great questions, everyone. If I didn't get to yours, [how to follow up]."

---

## CLOSE
"Thanks so much for joining. [Final thought]."

"Connect with me on [LinkedIn/Twitter] - I share [what you share]."

"Have a great [day/week]!"
```

## Short-Form Video (Reels/TikTok/Shorts, 30-60 sec)

```markdown
# [Viral-Worthy Hook]

**Target length:** 30-60 seconds
**Goal:** Reach, brand awareness

---

## HOOK (0:00-0:03)
[Pattern interrupt - make them stop scrolling]

Options:
- "POV: [Relatable scenario]"
- "The [topic] cheat code nobody talks about:"
- "Stop [common mistake]. Do this instead:"
- "[Number] second [topic] tip:"

---

## CONTENT (0:03-0:25)
[One single idea, delivered fast]

- Get to the point immediately
- Use movement/gestures
- On-screen text reinforces key points
- No fluff

---

## PAYOFF (0:25-0:30)
[The "aha" moment]

"And that's how you [outcome]."
OR
"Now you know [insight]."

---

## CTA (text overlay only)
"Follow for more [topic] tips"
OR
"Save this for later"
```

## Video Production Tips

```yaml
talking_head:
  framing:
    - Eyes at top third of frame
    - Headroom: small gap above head
    - Look at lens, not screen
    - Background: clean, professional

  delivery:
    - Energy 20% higher than normal
    - Vary pace and volume
    - Use hand gestures
    - Smile at key moments

  audio:
    - External mic essential
    - Reduce echo (soft surfaces)
    - Consistent volume
    - No background noise

screen_recording:
  setup:
    - Clean desktop
    - Close notifications
    - Zoom to 125-150%
    - Hide bookmarks bar
    - Incognito mode

  narration:
    - Describe what you're doing
    - "Now I'm going to..."
    - "Notice how..."
    - Pause before clicks

editing:
  pacing:
    - Cut dead air
    - Jump cuts OK for tutorials
    - 2-3 second rule (no shot longer without purpose)

  engagement:
    - Add B-roll every 30-60 sec
    - On-screen text for key points
    - Zoom for emphasis
    - Sound effects (subtle)
```

## Video Content Repurposing

```
Long-form YouTube video (10 min)
          ↓
├── 3-5 YouTube Shorts (60 sec clips)
├── 3-5 LinkedIn native videos (60-90 sec)
├── 3-5 Instagram Reels
├── 3-5 TikToks
├── Podcast audio episode
├── Blog post (transcript + enhancement)
├── Twitter/X thread (key points)
└── Quote graphics (5-10)
```
