# Blog Post Templates

Structures for different B2B blog post types.

## The How-To Guide

Best for: Tutorial content, step-by-step instructions

```markdown
# How to [Achieve Outcome] in [Timeframe/Steps]

**Meta:** Learn how to [outcome] with this step-by-step guide. Covers [topics] so you can [benefit].

## Introduction (150 words)
- Hook: Pain point or desire
- What they'll learn
- Why you're qualified to teach this

## Why [Topic] Matters
- Context and stakes
- Data point or stat
- What happens if they don't do this

## Prerequisites (if applicable)
- What they need before starting
- Tools, accounts, knowledge

## Step 1: [Action Verb] [Object]
- What to do
- Why it matters
- Example or screenshot
- Common mistake to avoid

## Step 2: [Action Verb] [Object]
[Same structure]

## Step 3: [Action Verb] [Object]
[Same structure]

## Step 4: [Action Verb] [Object]
[Same structure]

## Pro Tips
- Advanced tip 1
- Advanced tip 2
- Advanced tip 3

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Mistake 1 and how to fix it
- Mistake 2 and how to fix it

## Next Steps
- What to do after completing this
- Related resources
- CTA

## FAQ
Q: [Common question]
A: [Answer]
```

## The Listicle

Best for: Roundups, tips, tools, ideas

```markdown
# [Number] [Adjective] [Things] for [Audience/Goal]

**Meta:** Discover [number] [things] that [benefit]. Includes [specific items] and [bonus].

## Introduction (100 words)
- Why this list matters
- How you curated it
- What they'll walk away with

## 1. [Item Name]
**Best for:** [Use case]

[2-3 sentence description]

**Key features:**
- Feature 1
- Feature 2
- Feature 3

**Pricing:** [If applicable]

## 2. [Item Name]
[Same structure]

[Continue for all items]

## How to Choose
- Decision criteria 1
- Decision criteria 2
- Quick comparison table

## Conclusion
- Summary of top picks
- Recommendation by use case
- CTA
```

## Writing Tips

```yaml
hooks:
  - Start with a surprising statistic
  - Open with a question
  - Begin with a bold statement
  - Use a relevant anecdote
  - Paint a before/after picture

readability:
  - Paragraphs: 2-3 sentences max
  - Sentences: 20 words average
  - Use headers every 300 words
  - Include visuals every 500 words
  - Bullet points for lists of 3+

engagement:
  - Use "you" more than "we"
  - Include examples for every concept
  - Add internal links to related content
  - End sections with transitions
  - Use pattern interrupts (questions, quotes)
```
