## Engagement Hooks

### Question Starters
- "What's the worst advice you've received about [topic]?"
- "Complete this sentence: The best [professionals] always..."
- "Hot take or truth: [Statement]"
- "If you could go back and tell yourself one thing about [topic], what would it be?"

### Pattern Interrupts
- "Stop scrolling. This is important."
- "I'm going to get hate for this, but..."
- "Most [professionals] won't tell you this..."
- "Unpopular opinion incoming:"

### Closing CTAs
- "What would you add?"
- "Agree or disagree?"
- "Save this for later."
- "Tag someone who needs to see this."
- "Drop a [emoji] if this resonates."

## Carousel Templates

### The How-To Carousel
```
Slide 1: [Attention-grabbing title]
Slide 2: The problem
Slide 3: Why it matters
Slide 4-8: Steps 1-5
Slide 9: Summary
Slide 10: CTA + Follow prompt
```

### The Comparison Carousel
```
Slide 1: [X] vs [Y]: Which is better?
Slide 2: What is [X]?
Slide 3: Pros of [X]
Slide 4: Cons of [X]
Slide 5: What is [Y]?
Slide 6: Pros of [Y]
Slide 7: Cons of [Y]
Slide 8: Verdict
Slide 9: When to use each
Slide 10: CTA
```

    - Use numbers, questions, or bold statements
    - Create curiosity gap

  structure:
    - One idea per line
    - Lots of white space
    - Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)

  emojis:

  length:

    - Carousels: 1,200-1,500 characters
    - Videos: Tease in text, full content in video
